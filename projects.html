Knowledge-Based AI Agent for Raven’s Progressive Matrices (RPM)

Course: CS7637 Knowledge-Based AI (Georgia Tech)

Description: Developed an intelligent agent capable of solving Raven’s Progressive Matrices (RPM) problems using Knowledge-Based AI techniques. The agent converts images to black-and-white NumPy arrays and applies various strategies, including affine transformations, dark pixel ratio comparisons, bitwise transformations, and heuristic approaches like discard-and-choose. The agent achieved a performance score of 78.13% on standard RPM problems.

Key Strategies Used:

  • Affine transformations (for 2x2 matrices)
  • Dark Pixel Ratio (DPR) comparison
  • Bitwise transformations (for 3x3 matrices)
  • Heuristic "Discard and Choose" strategy

Outcome: Successfully solved 75 out of 96 problems on the Gradescope platform, demonstrating effective AI problem-solving strategies aligned with human analogical reasoning.
